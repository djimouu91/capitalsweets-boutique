Our Story

A grandmother's kitchen,
now at your doorstep

CapitalSweets was born from a simple love — the smell of orange blossom and honey filling a Algerian home. Every recipe has been passed down through generations, made with the same care, the same ingredients, the same patience.

Based in Ottawa, we bring authentic Algerian flavours to Canadian tables. Every piece is made fresh when you order — no preservatives, no shortcuts, just tradition.
